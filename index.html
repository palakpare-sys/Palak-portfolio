About me

Hello,

I'm Palak, a graphic designer based in Mumbai driven by creativity and curiosity. I am a quiet observer who prefers to let my work speak, I enjoy transforming observations into ideas and visual outcomes. As someone still in an experimental phase, I constantly like to explore different styles - from bold and expressive to clean and minimal. I embrace every project as a space to learn and evolve. Being a perfectionist, I pay close attention to detail, believing that even the smallest elements contribute to a meaningful and thoughtful design.
